There will be 8 Modules in This Course

Module 1: Blockchain Fundamentals

Introduction to blockchain technology, cryptography, distributed systems, and consensus mechanisms. Understand how blockchains work, different types of blockchains, and their real-world applications.

  • Blockchain Architecture & Components
  • Cryptography & Hash Functions
  • Consensus Mechanisms (PoW, PoS, DPoS)
  • Public vs Private Blockchains
  • Bitcoin & Cryptocurrency Basics

Module 2: Ethereum & Smart Contracts

Deep dive into Ethereum blockchain, EVM, and smart contract development. Learn Solidity programming language and build your first smart contracts.

  • Ethereum Architecture & EVM
  • Solidity Programming Language
  • Smart Contract Development
  • Gas Optimization Techniques
  • Development Tools (Hardhat, Truffle)
  • Testing & Debugging Contracts

Module 3: Web3 & DApp Development

Build decentralized applications connecting smart contracts with frontend interfaces. Master Web3.js, Ethers.js, and modern JavaScript frameworks.

  • Web3.js & Ethers.js Libraries
  • MetaMask & Wallet Integration
  • React for DApp Development
  • IPFS & Decentralized Storage
  • The Graph Protocol
  • User Authentication in Web3

Module 4: DeFi Development

Create decentralized finance applications including DEXs, lending protocols, yield farming, and liquidity pools. Understand DeFi economics and tokenomics.

  • DeFi Ecosystem Overview
  • ERC-20 Token Development
  • Automated Market Makers (AMM)
  • Lending & Borrowing Protocols
  • Yield Farming & Staking
  • Flash Loans & Arbitrage

Module 5: NFT & Digital Assets

Develop NFT smart contracts, marketplaces, and gaming applications. Learn ERC-721, ERC-1155 standards and create innovative NFT projects.

  • NFT Standards (ERC-721, ERC-1155)
  • NFT Marketplace Development
  • Metadata & IPFS Storage
  • Generative Art & Collections
  • NFT Gaming & Metaverse
  • Royalties & Secondary Sales

Module 6: Smart Contract Security

Master smart contract security, auditing techniques, and best practices. Learn to identify and prevent common vulnerabilities and attacks.

  • Common Vulnerabilities & Attacks
  • Security Best Practices
  • Auditing Tools & Techniques
  • OpenZeppelin Contracts
  • Formal Verification
  • Bug Bounty Programs

Module 7: Advanced Blockchain Development

Explore Layer 2 solutions, cross-chain development, and enterprise blockchain. Build on Polygon, Binance Smart Chain, and other platforms.

  • Layer 2 Solutions (Polygon, Arbitrum)
  • Cross-chain Bridges & Interoperability
  • Binance Smart Chain Development
  • Hyperledger Fabric
  • Oracles & Chainlink
  • Zero-Knowledge Proofs

Module 8: Blockchain Business & Career

Understand blockchain business models, regulations, and career opportunities. Build your portfolio and prepare for blockchain developer roles.

  • Blockchain Business Models
  • Tokenomics & ICO/IDO
  • Legal & Regulatory Aspects
  • DAO Development & Governance
  • Portfolio Development
  • Interview Preparation
